Project Description and Objectives
1. GreenGrid: Smart Energy Optimization
  • Description: A web-based dashboard to monitor and optimize energy usage in residential or commercial buildings using IoT sensors and AI.
  • Objectives:Reduce overall energy consumption by 15%. Implement real-time monitoring of energy usage.
2. EduBridge: Digital Learning for Remote Areas
  • Description: A mobile-first education platform designed for underprivileged or remote communities with limited access to formal education.
  • Objectives: Deliver offline-accessible learning modules for students in rural areas.
3. AgriAssist: AI-Powered Crop Advisor
  • Description: An app that uses satellite data and machine learning to help farmers make data-driven decisions on planting, irrigation, and fertilization.
  • Objectives: Increase crop yields by providing accurate predictions.
